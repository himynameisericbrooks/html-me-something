There once was a thimble named Purple & Purple was the shiniest thimble in the land of oOz.

And in the land of oOz there were also 13 dwarves. Their names were Sleepy, Sheepy, Weepy, Ugly, Hairy, Scary, Barry, Salivary, Glary, Starey, Contrary, KlicKcloK, and Dort. Dort and KlicKcloK were accidents. That is why their parents chose not to give them names that end in 'y'.

One day, Dort got his piloting licence. On his first solo flight, he crashed into a pinata! The pinata had been hanging high above the Elementarian Elementary School of the Elements for 3 years and no one could figure out how to bust it open.

Its one weakness was planes. So when Dort planed right into it, the pinata busted open and candy rained down on all of the children from left to right.

Each of the children joyfully ate the candy and then suffered from severe allergic reactions. They all lived mediocrely-ever-after.

The End?
